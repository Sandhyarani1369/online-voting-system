Election Rules & Instructions

Voting Rules

  • Each voter is allowed to vote only once.
  • Votes are confidential and cannot be changed after submission.
  • Only registered students are eligible to vote.
  • Fake registrations or fraudulent activities will lead to disqualification.

Instructions for Voters

  • Register using your student ID and email.
  • Login to the voting system during the election period.
  • Select your preferred candidate and submit your vote.
  • Check the Results Page after the voting ends.

How to Register as a Nominee?

  • Ensure you meet the eligibility criteria for the election.
  • Nominee Registration Form
  • Provide your campaign details and upload your profile picture.
  • Submit the form before the nomination deadline.
  • Wait for admin approval to appear on the ballot.
